Adventure

You are a student with a part time job in an old antique shop. You work just enough to cover your rent and are always balancing your time between studying and working. Your job includes dusting, organizing shelves and helping customers - on the rare occasion where they do come in. The shop is owned by a kind elderly man called Mr Jones, he pays you just above minimum wage and lives above the shop. You have been entrusted as a keyholder and see Mr Jones very rarely. Your adventure will start on what seems like an average day for you...
