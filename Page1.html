A regular day...

The harsh shrill of your alarm startles you awake. Out of bed you get. Into the shower. No uni today but just a shift in the antique shop 09:00-13:00 You start off what seems like a regular day with your usual breakfast of a bowl of muesli and fruit. You walk to the antique shop and when you arrive there is a dusty old box on the doorstep. similair to a charity shop people in this town seem to leave donations for some reason. this is nothing out of the ordinary and you grab your keys from your jacket pocket and enter the shop. The dusty old bell attached to the door jingles as you enter the shop, you cough as the dust particles invade your nostrils. Isn't it strange how quickly dust gathers in this little shop. Once you are settled and have put your jacket and bag in the back room you start up the til system, naturally it is an old system that takes ages to boot up. You begin to hear a strange, eerie noise coming from somewhere. Do you investigate this noise or ignore it and continue setting up for the day?
