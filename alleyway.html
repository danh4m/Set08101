Alleyway maze...

You had to run down the dark alleyway. The demons were fast approaching. As you enter the alley darkness surrounds you. It takes a good minute for your eyes to adjust before you can properly see again. Still in fear of being chased you continue running down the alley for another 5 minutes or so. The alley is long and relies on sunlight of which it doesnt get much at all.

Finally the alley forks off and comes to a junction. But you don't know where either of the routes go... Looking over your shoulder you see the demons catching up with you - still with that same expression of anger and a desire for blood. You have to pick a path - QUICKLY!
