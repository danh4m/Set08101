Flight for your life...

You made the wise decision of getting the out of there immediately. You barge past the demon and head out of the shop onto the street. A few passerbys glance at you but carry on walking. You start yelling at everyone telling them abou the demon! You receive odd looks and stares , a woman covers her childs eyes and leads them across the road away from you. Knowing that this terrifying demon is in the shop you make it a priority to put as much distance between you and it as possible. You begin sprinting as fast as you can. The pavements are busy and folk aren't getting out of the way, you run onto the road. After a relatively short sprint you realise that you will soon tire out and the demon could catch up with you.

There is a taxi rank a few blocks away and there is a bus stop nearby. Both options could help you escape! Which do you choose?
