You died...

You chose to ignore the ominous and unusual noise. But it continued to sound. The noise gets progressively louder and louder until you can no longer bear it. It feels like your head is caving in and there is an insurmountable amount of pressure on your ears. Blood starts dripping out of your ears and you feel your body getting weaker. Suddenly your legs give way and you are on the splintered old floorboards of the shops.

On your hands and knees you look up to the light coming in through the window, it slowly fades into oblivion. You are dead. Better luck next time.
