Bus ride...

You chose to head to the nearby bus station. Running quickly you see a bus just about to leave the station. PHEW. You just made it. Feeling safe but confused you take a window seat and slouch into it. The driving begins heading toward your home. You look out the window onto the streets to see demons, zombies and ghouls wandering amongst everyone else. No normal persons seem to be taking any notice of them at all.

The bus pulls onto your street and you can see your house. Your home is surrounded by zombies, they seem disturbed and angry. One zombie takes notice of your staring and begins sprinting toward the bus. It doesn't seem like you'll be able to go home. The bus drives off and you watch as the zombie continues it's pursuit. What now? You ask yourself. Your friends house is on the same bus route or you could head into town.
