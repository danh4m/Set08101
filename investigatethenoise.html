Investigate the noise...

You choose to investigate the noise, a wise choice. Trying to determine where it is coming from you walk around the dimly lit shop. The tall shelves loom over you menacingly casting their long shadow. The noise stops for a second, you halt and hear the noise of the floorboards creaking below you. The noise resumes and at this point it is quite clear to you where it is coming from. You head toward the door where the mysterious box was abandoned. As you open the door the little bell attached produces an out of tune ring. a small gust of wind blows in as the door widens open slowly, you don't notice anything else on the pavement, no leaves or cracks in the pavement. You can only focus on the box. There is a strange aura about it and it seems as though now that you have gone toward it you can do nothing but take it inside and see whats inside. You bend over to pick it up, wrap your fingers around the edges and pull it from the ground. The box rushes up with you as though it was empty. accidentally leaving the door ajar, you head back inside the shop carrying the box with no effort at all and place it on the shop counter. suddenly a powerful gust of wind slams the wooden door shut behind you. you jump out of your skin, what a fright that gave you. "It was just the wind" you tell yourself as you head to the other side of the counter for some scissors to cut the tape wrapped around the cardboard box. With one swift movement of the scissors the tape is cut and you are able to open the box. You move the cardboard flaps to either side to reveal a purple velvet cloth covering the contents of the box. You can hear blood pumping in your head, you are very tense. You can rip the cloth off as quick as you can or you can gently drag it off.
