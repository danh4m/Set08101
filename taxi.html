Taxi...

You chose to run to the taxi rank just a few blocks away. Arriving dripping with sweat you see that there is only one taxi. Desperate to escaper you get in and yell DRIVE. The taxi driver zooms off and you feel a brief sense of relief. Sweat rolls down your neck. You are safe... or so you thought. After a moment you look out the back windows to see hordes of zombies, demons and ghouls wandering the street. Oh no! There are dozens of people walking the street seemingly not taking any notice of them... strange.

Suddenly, the taxi swerves and you pay attention to the driver for the first time. Their black hood covers their face and their hands on the wheel are covered with black gloves. The rear view mirror appears to be missing. Feeling your seatbelt tighten, the taxi flips and rolls several times. You are hurt. The taxi driver turns their head, looks at you and says "It's time". Finally you are able to see their face with its skeletal features and soulless eyes. Death.

Death watches you as everything fades into darkness, into nothingness.
