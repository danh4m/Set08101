Secret...

You chose to keep this whole thing to yourself and not tell a single soul. That night you stayed at your friends. It seemed safe, it really did. You quietly had dinner with their family, watched a film and went to bed. It came to the early hours of the morning and you still couldn't sleep. Every slight little noise filled you with fear. Your mind kept thinking of the demon looming over you in the shop, ready to tear you apart with it's claws. Clenching your eyes shut you try hard to fall asleep. You feel a presence above you. Opening your eyes reveals the same demon as before. You try to scream but with a snap of its long gnarled fingers you cannot. You can only scream internally and that you do. You glance over to your friend in the other bed - totally unaware of what's happening here. If only you had told them.

With a painful swift movement of the demons claws it slits your throat. Blood gushes out and onto the bedsheets. It's excruciatingly painful for you. The demon drops your lifeless corpse and you are stuck watching from 3rd person. You watch your limp body crumple in a heap on the floor. The demon glides over toward your friend. They wake up and look over to see your body lying on the ground, before they get a chance to let out a scream or act the demon has violently detached their head from their body. Their skin goes pale and they too fall to the floor in a heap. You have no choice but to watch on helplessly.

The demon turns and looks at you with its evil eyes. It disappears and you are left roaming for eternity as a ghost. A ghost who got their friend killed.
