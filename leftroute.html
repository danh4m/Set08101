Left route...

You run down the route on the left as quick as you can. It is still dangerously dark and you trip as you try to get away. The demon is hot on your heels and seems to be catching up with you. You just hope that you've picked the right path...

But unfortunately you haven't... The alley comes to a dead end with brick walls of large buildings towering on all three sides. You anxiously look around to see if theres any windows or doors you could go through. Desperately you try to find something you could use as a weapon but it't no good. In your panicked fluster you weren't keeping an eye on the fast approaching demon. The evil demon attacks and violently claws at your head. It's screech is piercingly loud.

Painfully, everything fades into darkness. It's unlucky that you ridnt pick the right path. You are dead.
