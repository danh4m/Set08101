RIP the cloth...

You choose to vigourously rip the cloth out of the box. A powerful energy launches you backwards crashing through the door of the shop. Crashing through the doors and skidding across the wooden planked floor, you find yourself at the foot of a tall shelving unit. 'What the fuck was that?' you think to yourself. The tall shelving unit next to you is wobbling, heavy-looking antiques balance precariously on the edges. As you are about too get up you see it falling. A brass clock that had been sitting on the top shelf undisturbed for years was falling straight for your head. Time slows down and you squint at the clock almost making out the engraving underneath.

You try to get your arms up to catch the clock or block it from hitting your head but its too late. Time resumes. The clock is picking up speed and hurtling directly toward your skull. *CRACK*. Everything goes black. That was the sound of the antique clock hitting your skull at pace.

You are dead.
