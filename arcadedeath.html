Arcade Death...

You were unable to beat the arcade. You plead with the man to just let you in. You try explaining everything to him but he's having none of it. You beg and you beg but he won't let you in. Nothing you say to him has any impact on his decision. There's nothing you can do. The man goes to bolt the door shut. Out of sheer desperation you attempt to barge past him but it's no use. He turns and looks at you with a frown .THWACK.

The man hits you with a right hook and you fall to the floor in a heap. Barely conscious with your head on the concrete ground you look up to see the dark demon figures surrounding you. The demons harmonise their death chant and begin disemboweling you. Blood gushes everywhere.

If only you'd been a wee bit better at video games.
