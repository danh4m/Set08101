Town Centre...

You head to the town centre in the hope that it will be busy so you won't get attacked. Right enough the town is busy and not just with the living. Ghosts and demons glide around the streets aimlessly. No one bats an eyelid seemingly. Out of the corner of your eye you see the same demon from the shop. It looks angry and lets out a terrible roar. Upon hearing this, other demons turn toward you with the same angry expression.

time to run... you sprint toward a large shopping mall. To access the mall you must pass through a video game arcade. Upon reaching the entrance a man stops you and says "I won't let you in unless you challenge one of my games".

You don't have enough time to explain everything to him and theres no way you could squeeze past him. To the left you see a dark alleyway where you could run to. What do you do?
