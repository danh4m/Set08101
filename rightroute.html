Right route...

You run down the route on the right as quick as you can. It is still dangerously dark and you trip as you try to get away. The demon is hot on your heels and seems to be catching up with you. You just hope that you've picked the right path...

It seems as though you have! The alleyway leads to an open door to a stairwell. You get inside with enough time to barricade the doors behind you. hew. You hear the noise of the demon outside clawing at the door trying to get in. But it can't. For now you are safe. You follow the stairwell up to the very top where it leads out onto a large roof looking over the town. On the roof there is a small helicopter - an escape! You don't know how to fly a helicopter but you see no other choice. You get into the helicopter and stare at the instructions.

Without thinking about it you begin to take off and are flying through the air. You fly away, far away, as far away from that wretched town as possible.
