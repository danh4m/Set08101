Introduction

Welcome to the introduction page of my choose your own adventure game where you are the main character. To begin playing your adventure then head to the Adventure page.

The adventure story has multiple possible outcomes which you may reach depending on the choices that you make. There are a total of 8 possible ways to die in this game. You are always welcome to take a few steps back in your story and change your fate. The choices you make affect the outcomes and pages that you reach The aim is to survive in this horror themed adventure story! Good Luck.

I hope you enjoy playing - Dan
